 Find A Book

Find a book - it is so easy.
Now you has access to millions of free books online.
Available on the App Store

Key features

  • Seeks ebooks in the OPDS catalogs
  • Downloads the found books
  • Opens the downloaded books in any eReader
  • Keeps a history of search
  • Lets you to add any other OPDS catalog

And even more

  • Global search across all sources
  • Customizable search terms
  • Full details of a found book
  • Search in iTunes, Google Books
  • Open-Source Licenses
