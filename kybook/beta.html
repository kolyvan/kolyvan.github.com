Release Description

  • Fixed an issue with the inability to open some epubs.
  • Added an option to customize the page footer.
  • Improved support of LitRes.ru
  • Added remembering of the current tab of the information screen.
  • Improved support of Speed Reading.
  • Drop Cap (only in fb2 book, turns on in the settings).
  • Fixed errors related to the device orientation change.
