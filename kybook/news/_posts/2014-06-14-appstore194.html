KyBook v1.9.4 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Integration with LitRes.ru service
  • Integration with Samizdat service (samlib.ru)
  • Added 'Share link' feature for Cloud Storage
  • Added option to show nested bookshelves
  • Added FiraSans font
  • Added downloadable PT-Sans and PT-Serif fonts
  • Various fixes and improvements
