--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 2.0.1 ---

KyBook v2.0.1 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • iPhone6/6+ Support.
  • Improved presentation of footnotes in FB2 books on iPad.
  • Displaying of images by double tap in FB2 books.
  • Page turning by tap in the scroll mode (with and without animation).
  • Bookshelf renaming.
  • Indication of the current chapter in the table of contents.
  • Integration with Yandex.Dictionary.
  • IDLE timer is disabled on File Transfer screen.
  • Support of a dictionary (menu item: define) in DJVU.
  • Added display of a chapter title to PDF/DJVU page.
  • New design of OPDS Catalogs screen.
  • Restored the ability to share all the notes at once.
  • Fixed a performance problem.
  • Fixed a bug with downloading audiobooks from LitRes.
