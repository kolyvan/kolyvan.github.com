--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 1.9.2 ---

KyBook v1.9.2 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Redesign of the translation function.
  • Added the feature to display a sentence during Speed Reading.
  • Added AlarmClock function.
  • Improved support for OCR layer in DJVU books.
  • Fixed "Out of memory" error for FB2 books with lots of images.
  • Fixed an issue with playback audio/TTS during an incoming call.
