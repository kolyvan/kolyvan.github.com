--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 1.8.8 ---

KyBook v1.8.8 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Streaming audiobooks from Yandex.Disk, Dropbox and Network Folders.
  • Rate control for audiobooks.
  • Editable settings of OPDS Catalogs and Network Folders.
  • Menu item "Select All" in a bookshelf.
  • Search by tags during searching books by title or author.
  • Display synchronization progress in the sync settings.
  • Improved support of Speed Reading.
  • Improved support of FB2 books which violate FictionBook2.1 standard.
  • Optimized downloading of ebooks from OPDS Catalogs and Websites.
  • Disable autohiding of a search bar in OPDS Catalog.
  • Idle timer is disabled during synchronization.
  • Fixed an issue with editing of bookmark.
  • Fixed an issue with audiobooks in landscape orientation.
  • Fixed an issue with adding a bookshelf using Library Browser in iOS6.
  • Fixed an issue with showing of bookcover from locked password-protected bookshelf.
  • Fixed "Unable to parse OPDS Feed.".
