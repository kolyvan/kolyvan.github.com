--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 1.7.7 ---

KyBook v1.7.7 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Alternative EPUB rendering engine.
  • Added synchronization via Dropbox, Yandex.Disk or Network Folders.
  • Added support for nested bookshelves.
  • Improved support of Cloud Storage and Network Folders.
  • Improved support of e-books in CHM and PDF formats.
  • Improved support of OPDS catalogs.
